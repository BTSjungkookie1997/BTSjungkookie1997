Our Plants Collection

Ficus

Ficus plants are known for their attractive, glossy leaves and are great for indoor decor.

Succulent

Succulents are low-maintenance plants that retain water in their thick leaves, perfect for dry conditions.

Snake Plant

Snake plants are resilient and can tolerate low light and irregular watering, making them great for beginners.

Peace Lily

Peace lilies are known for their beautiful white blooms and air-purifying qualities, ideal for indoor spaces.

Aloe Vera

Aloe Vera is a popular succulent known for its healing properties, especially in skincare.

Spider Plant

Spider plants are hardy, easy-to-grow plants with long, arching leaves and air-purifying benefits.

Orchid

Orchids are exotic flowers known for their elegant blooms and are often used in decorative arrangements.

Monstera

Monstera plants are known for their unique, split leaves, making them a trendy choice for home decor.

Lavender

Lavender is an aromatic herb known for its calming fragrance and purple flowers, often used in relaxation therapies.

Rose

Roses are classic flowers with a variety of colors and are loved for their fragrance and beauty.
